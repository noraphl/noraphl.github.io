About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Education at The University of Hong Kong, specialising in the intersection of artificial intelligence, learning analytics, and culturally relevant education within STEAM disciplines.

My research explores how AI can be leveraged to create more equitable and personalized learning experiences, with a particular focus on supporting underrepresented students in STEAM fields. I am passionate about developing learning technologies that respect and incorporate diverse cultural perspectives.
